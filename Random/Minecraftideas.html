New Biome - Crystal

        Crystalwood trees :

                They can make crystalwood rods. Using a crystalwood rod instead of a stick in crafting adds efficiency to it while a weapon adds sharpness. The crystalwood can make a 5 attack damage crystalwood sword.

        Crystalite ore :

                Makes tools that are 10% better than diamond tools. It is 10% rarer than diamond.

Snowy variants of the biome can be found.

New item - Fireball  

        Made out of a 1 fire charge, 2 blaze powder and 1 ghast tear (recipe gives 6 fireballs). Can be thrown.

New mob - Cave Slime

        Drops cave slimeballs. Cave slimeballs act exactly the same as slimeballs (only a different texture). Spawns in cave systems. Will stick to ceiling and drop down onto your head and will remain there, dealing constant damage of ½ a heart per ¾ second, until knocked off. It has 2 ½ hearts of health. Will die in one hit by any sword. The slime doesn’t split. It has only one size: medium. (2 in game code).

New Item - Phoenix feather  

        Drops from a Phoenix. Can be used to enhance tools by replacing lapis in an enchantment table. Has a max enchantment level of 45 (instead of the usual 30).

New Physics  

        If two left-click functioning tools are equipped in both hands, the offhand item will be used by right-click. (i.e. functioning dual-wielding of swords)
