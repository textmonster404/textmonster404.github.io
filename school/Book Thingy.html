Reading Post Back

I am reading the Dear Dumb Diary series by Jim Benton.  This series of books is a graphic novel about a girl whoose name is Jamie Kelly.  I am wanting very badly to read the entire series soon.  The theme goes like this: Jamie has some issues at her school.  (A classic school story right?)  The antagonist of the story is the most beautiful girl in the school: Angeline.  The design of the story is a lot like the book series "Diary of a Wimpy Kid".  It may be very similar to it, but it is much more fun to read.  It is an exciting story with great humor and full of energy.
